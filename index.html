Scoring rules

• Win +3  ·  Tie +1
• Goal difference (winner only): +1 per goal of margin — a 3–1 win pays +3 (win) +2 (GD)
• Clean sheet: +1 for conceding zero (a 0–0 tie pays both sides +1 tie +1 CS)
• Underdog goals: +1 for every goal a bottom-16 team scores on a top-16 team, win or lose
Only finished matches count in the standings — live matches show provisional points marked LIVE.
